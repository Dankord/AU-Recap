What is the Website all about

This website was established by the students of ICT 11-A from the Apolinario Mabini Campus with the primary objective of providing convenient access to video lessons to their peers who may have missed them or wish to review them for better understanding. It is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to all students of Arellano University - Apolinario Mabini Campus. All information provided by users on the website will be kept secure, and their privacy will be respected and valued. We sincerely hope that you find this website useful in your pursuit of knowledge, and that it enhances your learning journey.
